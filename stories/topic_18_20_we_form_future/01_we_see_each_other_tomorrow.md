## Story

Vivo aquí.
*I-live here.*

Daniel vive aquí.
*Daniel lives here.*

María vive aquí.
*María lives here.*

Lina no vive aquí.
*Lina no lives here.*

Pero vivimos.
*But we-live.*

Vivimos siempre.
*We-live always.*

Yo prefiero comer.
*I  prefer  to-eat.*

María prefiere dormir.
*María prefers to-sleep.*

Daniel prefiere venir.
*Daniel prefers to-come.*

Pero preferimos.
*But we-prefer.*

¿A dónde vas?
*To where you-go?*

Yo voy.
*I  go.*

Yo voy esta noche.
*I  go this  night.*

María va.
*María goes.*

Daniel va.
*Daniel goes.*

Vamos.
*We-go.*

Vamos a comer.
*We-go to eat.*

Vamos esta noche.
*We-go this night.*

Tengo que ir ahora.
*I-have that go now.*

Tienes que ir esta noche.
*You-have that go this night.*

Tenemos que ir mañana.
*We-have that go tomorrow.*

¿Cuándo vienes?
*When you-come?*

Vengo más tarde.
*I-come more late.*

Vengo pronto.
*I-come soon.*

Vienen mañana.
*They-come tomorrow.*

Venimos esta noche.
*We-come this night.*

Yo veo.
*I  see.*

La veo.
*Her I-see.*

La veo más tarde.
*Her I-see more late.*

La veo esta noche.
*Her I-see this night.*

Las veo pronto.
*Them I-see soon.*

Vamos a verla.
*We-go to to-see-her.*

¿Vas a verlo pronto?
*You-go to to-see-him soon?*

¿Por qué no vas a vernos?
*Why no you-go to to-see-us?*

Nos vemos.
*Each-other we-see.*

Nos vemos mañana.
*Each-other we-see tomorrow.*

Nos vemos la semana que viene.
*Each-other we-see the week that comes.*

Hablamos.
*We-speak.*

Hablamos pronto.
*We-speak soon.*

Hablamos la semana que viene.
*We-speak the week that comes.*

Ciao.
*Bye.*

I live here. Daniel lives here. María lives here. Lina doesn't live here. But we live. We always live. I prefer to eat. María prefers to sleep. Daniel prefers to come. But we prefer. Where are you going? I'm going. I'm going tonight. María is going. Daniel is going. We're going. We're going to eat. We're going tonight. I have to go now. You have to go tonight. We have to go tomorrow. When are you coming? I'm coming later. I'm coming soon. They're coming tomorrow. We're coming tonight. I see. I see her. I'm seeing her later. I'm seeing her tonight. I'm seeing them soon. We're going to see her. Are you going to see him soon? Why aren't you going to see us? See you. See you tomorrow. See you next week. Let's speak. Let's speak soon. Let's speak next week. Bye.

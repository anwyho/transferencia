## Story

Me he perdido.
*Me I-have lost.*

Yo me he perdido.
*I  me have lost.*

María se ha perdido.
*María herself has lost.*

Daniel se ha perdido.
*Daniel himself has lost.*

Lina no se ha perdido.
*Lina no herself has lost.*

Nos hemos perdido.
*Us we-have lost.*

Nosotros nos hemos perdido.
*We us have lost.*

Te has perdido.
*You yourself-have lost.*

Tú te has perdido.
*You yourself-have lost.*

¿Te has perdido?
*Yourself have-you lost?*

He entendido el problema.
*I-have understood the problem.*

¿Has entendido el sistema?
*Have-you understood the system?*

¿Has sentido el aroma?
*Have-you felt the aroma?*

¿Has entendido el programa?
*Have-you understood the program?*

Yo doy.
*I  give.*

Lo doy.
*It I-give.*

Te lo doy.
*You it I-give.*

Te lo doy mañana.
*You it I-give tomorrow.*

Me lo dan.
*Me it they-give.*

Ellos me lo dan.
*They me it give.*

Nos lo dan.
*Us it they-give.*

¿Nos lo dan mañana?
*Us it they-give tomorrow?*

¿Ustedes nos lo dan?
*You-guys us it give?*

Quiero dar.
*I-want to-give.*

Te lo quiero dar.
*You it I-want to-give.*

Me habla.
*Me he-speaks.*

Me lo habla.
*Me it he-speaks.*

Me lo compra.
*Me it he-buys.*

Te lo compro.
*You it I-buy.*

¿Te lo compro?
*You it I-buy?*

Me cocina.
*Me he-cooks.*

¿Me puedes cocinar algo?
*Me you-can to-cook  something?*

Cocino.
*I-cook.*

He cocinado.
*I-have cooked.*

He cocinado a las seis.
*I-have cooked at the six.*

Hemos cocinado a las ocho.
*We-have cooked at the eight.*

Me quedo aquí.
*Me I-stay here.*

Me quedo aquí hasta las diez.
*Me I-stay here until the ten.*

Nos quedamos hasta las nueve.
*Us we-stay  until the nine.*

Te quedas aquí.
*You yourself-stay here.*

¿Te quedas aquí?
*Yourself-stay you here?*

Yo me quedo.
*I  me stay.*

Hasta más tarde.
*Until more late.*

Hasta mañana.
*Until tomorrow.*

Hasta la vista.
*Until the view.*

## Free English translation

I've got lost. I (emph) have got lost. María has got lost. Daniel has got lost. Lina hasn't got lost. We have got lost. WE have got lost. You've got lost. YOU have got lost. Have you got lost? I've understood the problem. Have you understood the system? Have you smelt the aroma? Have you understood the program? I give. I give it. I give it to you. I'll give it to you tomorrow. They give it to me. THEY give it to me. They give it to us. Are they giving it to us tomorrow? Are YOU GUYS giving it to us? I want to give. I want to give it to you. He speaks to me. He speaks to me about it. He buys it for me. I buy it for you. Shall I buy it for you? He cooks for me. Can you cook something for me? I cook. I have cooked. I have cooked at six. We have cooked at eight. I'm staying here. I'm staying here until ten. We're staying until nine. You're staying here. Are you staying here? I (emph) am staying. Until later. Until tomorrow. Until we see each other.

## Story

María tiene un problema.
*María has a problem.*

Daniel tiene un problema importante.
*Daniel has a problem important.*

Lina no tiene un problema.
*Lina no has a problem.*

Yo sé.
*I  know.*

Lo sé.
*It I-know.*

María sabe.
*María knows.*

Pero Daniel no sabe.
*But Daniel no knows.*

Daniel no sabe qué hacer.
*Daniel no knows what to-do.*

Yo hago.
*I  do.*

Yo lo hago.
*I  it I-do.*

María lo hace.
*María it she-does.*

Lina lo hace constantemente.
*Lina it she-does constantly.*

Yo vengo.
*I  come.*

Vengo a comer.
*I-come to eat.*

Daniel viene.
*Daniel comes.*

Daniel viene más tarde.
*Daniel comes more late.*

Lina viene a comer con Daniel.
*Lina comes to eat with Daniel.*

Pero yo salgo ahora.
*But I  leave now.*

Salgo a comer.
*I-leave to eat.*

Yo pongo.
*I  put.*

Yo lo pongo.
*I  it I-put.*

Yo supongo que sí.
*I  suppose that yes.*

Tengo que comer.
*I-have that eat.*

Tengo que hacerlo.
*I-have that to-do-it.*

Tengo que verla.
*I-have that to-see-her.*

Quiero verla.
*I-want to-see-her.*

Puedo verla.
*I-can to-see-her.*

Debo verla.
*I-must to-see-her.*

Voy a verla.
*I-go to to-see-her.*

María quiere saber qué hago.
*María wants to-know what I-do.*

Daniel quiere saber qué hace Lina.
*Daniel wants to-know what does Lina.*

Lina quiere saber qué tiene Daniel.
*Lina wants to-know what has Daniel.*

Yo no quiero invitarlo.
*I  no want to-invite-him.*

Pero ella sí.
*But she yes.*

Ella quiere invitarlo.
*She wants to-invite-him.*

Cuándo viene Lina.
*When comes Lina.*

¿Cuándo vienes a comer?
*When you-come to eat?*

Vengo más tarde.
*I-come more late.*

Naturalmente.
*Naturally.*

## Free English translation

María has a problem. Daniel has an important problem. Lina doesn't have a problem. I know. I know it. María knows. But Daniel doesn't know. Daniel doesn't know what to do. I do. I do it. María does it. Lina does it constantly. I come. I come to eat. Daniel comes. Daniel comes later. Lina comes to eat with Daniel. But I leave now. I'm going out to eat. I put. I put it. I suppose so. I have to eat. I have to do it. I have to see her. I want to see her. I can see her. I must see her. I'm going to see her. María wants to know what I do. Daniel wants to know what Lina does. Lina wants to know what Daniel has. I don't want to invite him. But she does. She wants to invite him. When is Lina coming? When are you coming to eat? I'll come later. Naturally.

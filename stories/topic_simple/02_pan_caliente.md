## Story

Es la mañana.
*Is the morning.*

Daniel tiene hambre.
*Daniel has hunger.*

—Quiero pan —dice Daniel.
*—I-want bread —says Daniel.*

Daniel sale de la casa.
*Daniel leaves from the house.*

Camina por la calle.
*Walks along the street.*

La calle es tranquila.
*The street is calm.*

Daniel va a la panadería.
*Daniel goes to the bakery.*

La panadería se llama "El Sol".
*The bakery self calls "The Sun".*

Daniel entra.
*Daniel enters.*

—Buenos días —dice Daniel.
*—Good days —says Daniel.*

—Buenos días —dice la panadera.
*—Good days —says the baker(fem).*

—¿Qué quieres? —pregunta ella.
*—What you-want? —asks she.*

—Quiero pan, por favor.
*—I-want bread, please.*

—¿Pan blanco o pan integral?
*—Bread white or bread whole-grain?*

—Pan blanco. Y caliente.
*—Bread white. And hot.*

La panadera sonríe.
*The baker smiles.*

—El pan está muy caliente. Acaba de salir.
*—The bread is very hot. It-just-came of to-leave.*

Daniel huele el pan.
*Daniel smells the bread.*

Huele muy bien.
*It-smells very well.*

—Quiero dos panes.
*—I-want two breads.*

—Aquí tienes.
*—Here you-have.*

Daniel paga.
*Daniel pays.*

—Gracias.
*—Thanks.*

—De nada. Buen día.
*—Of nothing. Good day.*

Daniel sale con el pan.
*Daniel leaves with the bread.*

El pan está caliente en sus manos.
*The bread is hot in his hands.*

Daniel camina rápido.
*Daniel walks fast.*

Quiere comer ya.
*He-wants to-eat already.*

Llega a la casa.
*Arrives at the house.*

—¡Pan! —grita María.
*—Bread! —shouts María.*

—Pan caliente —dice Daniel.
*—Bread hot —says Daniel.*

Comen juntos.
*They-eat together.*

El pan es muy bueno.
*The bread is very good.*

—Mmm —dice María.
*—Mmm —says María.*

—Mmm —dice Daniel.
*—Mmm —says Daniel.*

## Free English translation

It's the morning. Daniel is hungry. "I want bread," says Daniel. Daniel leaves
the house. He walks down the street. The street is quiet. Daniel goes to the
bakery. The bakery is called "The Sun". Daniel enters. "Good morning," says
Daniel. "Good morning," says the baker. "What would you like?" she asks. "I'd
like bread, please." "White bread or whole-grain bread?" "White bread. And
hot." The baker smiles. "The bread is very hot. It just came out." Daniel
smells the bread. It smells very good. "I'd like two loaves." "Here you go."
Daniel pays. "Thank you." "You're welcome. Have a good day." Daniel leaves
with the bread. The bread is hot in his hands. Daniel walks quickly. He wants
to eat already. He arrives at the house. "Bread!" shouts María. "Hot bread,"
says Daniel. They eat together. The bread is very good. "Mmm," says María.
"Mmm," says Daniel.

## Story

Ayer fue sábado.
*Yesterday was Saturday.*

Mi mamá y yo fuimos al mercado.
*My mom and I we-went to-the market.*

El mercado está cerca.
*The market is near.*

Caminamos juntas.
*We-walked together(fem).*

Hacía sol.
*It-was-doing sun.*

—¿Qué vamos a comprar? —pregunté.
*—What we-go to to-buy? —I-asked.*

—Frutas y verduras —dijo mi mamá.
*—Fruits and vegetables —said my mom.*

Llegamos al mercado.
*We-arrived to-the market.*

El mercado estaba lleno.
*The market was full.*

Había mucha gente.
*There-was much people.*

Mi mamá compró tomates.
*My mom bought tomatoes.*

Compró manzanas rojas.
*She-bought apples red.*

—¿Quieres una manzana? —me preguntó.
*—You-want an apple? —me she-asked.*

—Sí, por favor.
*—Yes, please.*

Comí la manzana.
*I-ate the apple.*

Estaba dulce.
*It-was sweet.*

Vimos a un señor con un perro.
*We-saw a man with a dog.*

El perro era grande.
*The dog was big.*

—Mamá, ¿puedo tocarlo?
*—Mom, can-I to-touch-him?*

—Pregúntale al señor.
*—Ask-him to-the man.*

—Señor, ¿puedo tocar a su perro?
*—Sir, can-I to-touch your dog?*

—Sí, claro.
*—Yes, of-course.*

Toqué al perro.
*I-touched the dog.*

Era suave.
*He-was soft.*

El perro me lamió la mano.
*The dog me licked the hand.*

Reí mucho.
*I-laughed a-lot.*

Compramos pan y queso.
*We-bought bread and cheese.*

Compramos flores para la casa.
*We-bought flowers for the house.*

Volvimos a casa.
*We-returned to home.*

Llegamos cansadas pero felices.
*We-arrived tired(fem) but happy.*

Comimos juntas.
*We-ate together(fem).*

Fue un buen día.
*It-was a good day.*

## Free English translation

Yesterday was Saturday. My mom and I went to the market. The market is
nearby. We walked together. It was sunny. "What are we going to buy?" I
asked. "Fruits and vegetables," said my mom. We got to the market. The market
was full. There were many people. My mom bought tomatoes. She bought red
apples. "Do you want an apple?" she asked me. "Yes, please." I ate the apple.
It was sweet. We saw a man with a dog. The dog was big. "Mom, can I pet him?"
"Ask the man." "Sir, can I pet your dog?" "Yes, of course." I petted the dog.
He was soft. The dog licked my hand. I laughed a lot. We bought bread and
cheese. We bought flowers for the house. We went back home. We arrived tired
but happy. We ate together. It was a good day.

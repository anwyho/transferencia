## Story

Es de noche.
*Is of night.*

El niño está en su cama.
*The boy is in his bed.*

La luna brilla.
*The moon shines.*

La luna entra por la ventana.
*The moon enters through the window.*

—Buenas noches, luna.
*—Good nights, moon.*

El niño mira su cuarto.
*The boy looks-at his room.*

Hay un oso pequeño.
*There-is a bear small.*

—Buenas noches, oso.
*—Good nights, bear.*

Hay un libro.
*There-is a book.*

—Buenas noches, libro.
*—Good nights, book.*

Hay una silla roja.
*There-is a chair red.*

—Buenas noches, silla.
*—Good nights, chair.*

Hay una lámpara.
*There-is a lamp.*

—Buenas noches, lámpara.
*—Good nights, lamp.*

Hay un perro debajo de la cama.
*There-is a dog underneath of the bed.*

El perro se llama Rex.
*The dog self calls Rex.*

—Buenas noches, Rex.
*—Good nights, Rex.*

Rex mueve la cola.
*Rex moves the tail.*

Hay una madre en la puerta.
*There-is a mother in the door.*

—Buenas noches, mamá.
*—Good nights, mom.*

—Buenas noches, mi amor.
*—Good nights, my love.*

La mamá apaga la luz.
*The mom turns-off the light.*

El cuarto está oscuro.
*The room is dark.*

Pero la luna brilla.
*But the moon shines.*

El niño cierra los ojos.
*The boy closes the eyes.*

Sueña con la luna.
*He-dreams with the moon.*

Sueña con el oso.
*He-dreams with the bear.*

Sueña con Rex.
*He-dreams with Rex.*

Y duerme.
*And he-sleeps.*

—Buenas noches a todos.
*—Good nights to all.*

## Free English translation

It's nighttime. The boy is in his bed. The moon is shining. The moon comes in
through the window. "Goodnight, moon." The boy looks at his room. There is a
little bear. "Goodnight, bear." There is a book. "Goodnight, book." There is
a red chair. "Goodnight, chair." There is a lamp. "Goodnight, lamp." There is
a dog under the bed. The dog is called Rex. "Goodnight, Rex." Rex wags his
tail. There is a mother in the doorway. "Goodnight, mom." "Goodnight, my
love." Mom turns off the light. The room is dark. But the moon is shining.
The boy closes his eyes. He dreams about the moon. He dreams about the bear.
He dreams about Rex. And he sleeps. "Goodnight, everyone."

## Story

Es lunes.
*Is Monday.*

Llueve mucho.
*It-rains a-lot.*

Lina está en la casa.
*Lina is in the house.*

Lina mira por la ventana.
*Lina looks through the window.*

Las gotas caen.
*The drops fall.*

Las gotas son grandes.
*The drops are big.*

El cielo está gris.
*The sky is gray.*

—No me gusta la lluvia —dice Lina.
*—No me pleases the rain —says Lina.*

Lina quiere salir.
*Lina wants to-leave.*

Pero no puede.
*But no she-can.*

Su mamá llega.
*Her mom arrives.*

—¿Qué pasa, Lina?
*—What happens, Lina?*

—Quiero ir al parque.
*—I-want to-go to-the park.*

—Hoy no. Llueve mucho.
*—Today no. It-rains a-lot.*

Lina está triste.
*Lina is sad.*

Su mamá le sonríe.
*Her mom to-her smiles.*

—Vamos a jugar adentro.
*—Let's go to to-play inside.*

—¿A qué jugamos?
*—At what we-play?*

—A los dibujos.
*—At the drawings.*

Lina toma sus colores.
*Lina takes her colors.*

Su mamá toma papel.
*Her mom takes paper.*

Dibujan juntas.
*They-draw together.*

Lina dibuja un sol.
*Lina draws a sun.*

Su mamá dibuja un árbol.
*Her mom draws a tree.*

—Mira, mamá, un sol grande.
*—Look, mom, a sun big.*

—Es un sol bonito.
*—Is a sun pretty.*

Afuera llueve.
*Outside it-rains.*

Adentro, hace calor.
*Inside, it-makes heat.*

Lina ya no está triste.
*Lina already no is sad.*

—Mamá, te quiero.
*—Mom, you I-love.*

—Yo también te quiero.
*—I also you I-love.*

Y la lluvia sigue cayendo.
*And the rain keeps falling.*

## Free English translation

It's Monday. It's raining a lot. Lina is in the house. Lina looks through the
window. The drops fall. The drops are big. The sky is gray. "I don't like the
rain," says Lina. Lina wants to go out. But she can't. Her mom arrives.
"What's wrong, Lina?" "I want to go to the park." "Not today. It's raining a
lot." Lina is sad. Her mom smiles at her. "Let's play inside." "What shall we
play?" "Drawing." Lina takes her colors. Her mom takes paper. They draw
together. Lina draws a sun. Her mom draws a tree. "Look, mom, a big sun."
"It's a pretty sun." Outside, it rains. Inside, it's warm. Lina isn't sad
anymore. "Mom, I love you." "I love you too." And the rain keeps falling.

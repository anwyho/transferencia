## Story

María tiene un gato.
*María has a  cat.*

El gato se llama Tito.
*The cat self calls Tito.*

Tito es pequeño.
*Tito is small.*

Tito es blanco y negro.
*Tito is white and black.*

María quiere a Tito.
*María loves to Tito.*

Pero hoy, Tito no está.
*But today, Tito no is.*

María busca a Tito.
*María searches for Tito.*

—¿Tito? ¿Dónde estás?
*—Tito? Where you-are?*

María busca en la casa.
*María searches in the house.*

María busca en la cocina.
*María searches in the kitchen.*

Tito no está en la cocina.
*Tito no is in the kitchen.*

María busca en el jardín.
*María searches in the garden.*

Tito no está en el jardín.
*Tito no is in the garden.*

María está triste.
*María is sad.*

—¡Tito! ¡Tito!
*—Tito! Tito!*

Daniel llega a la casa.
*Daniel arrives at the house.*

—María, ¿qué pasa?
*—María, what happens?*

—Tito no está. No lo encuentro.
*—Tito no is. No him I-find.*

—Vamos a buscarlo.
*—Let's go to to-search-him.*

María y Daniel buscan juntos.
*María and Daniel search together.*

Buscan en la sala.
*They-search in the living-room.*

Buscan debajo de la mesa.
*They-search underneath of the table.*

Buscan detrás del sofá.
*They-search behind of-the sofa.*

Y allí está Tito.
*And there is Tito.*

Tito duerme detrás del sofá.
*Tito sleeps behind of-the sofa.*

—¡Tito!
*—Tito!*

Tito abre los ojos.
*Tito opens the eyes.*

Tito mira a María.
*Tito looks at María.*

—Miau.
*—Meow.*

María está feliz.
*María is happy.*

María toma a Tito en sus brazos.
*María takes Tito in her arms.*

—Tito, te quiero.
*—Tito, you I-love.*

Tito ronronea.
*Tito purrs.*

Y todo está bien.
*And everything is fine.*

## Free English translation

María has a cat. The cat is called Tito. Tito is small. Tito is white and
black. María loves Tito. But today, Tito isn't here. María looks for Tito.
"Tito? Where are you?" María looks in the house. María looks in the kitchen.
Tito isn't in the kitchen. María looks in the garden. Tito isn't in the
garden. María is sad. "Tito! Tito!" Daniel arrives at the house. "María,
what's wrong?" "Tito isn't here. I can't find him." "Let's go look for him."
María and Daniel search together. They search in the living room. They search
under the table. They search behind the sofa. And there is Tito. Tito is
sleeping behind the sofa. "Tito!" Tito opens his eyes. Tito looks at María.
"Meow." María is happy. María picks Tito up in her arms. "Tito, I love you."
Tito purrs. And everything is fine.

# Lesson 75

## Summary
We use the mood tense to turn words like *donde*, *como*, *que*, *cuando* into "wherever / however / whatever / whenever", and we meet the irregular mood form of *ser*: *sea*.

## Rules
1. **Positive informal command of *hacer*** is irregular: *haz* (not *hace*). *Haz* = do it / make it. *Hazlo* = do it.
2. The "wherever / however / whatever / whenever" trick: take a word like *donde* (where), *como* (how), *que* (what), *cuando* (when), follow it with a verb, and put that verb in the mood tense. The English suffix *-ever* is built into the Spanish mood-tense flip.
3. *Donde quieres* = where you want (a known place). *Donde quieras* = wherever you want (any place).
4. *Hazlo donde quieras* = do it wherever you want.
5. *Como te gusta* = how you like it. *Como te guste* = however you like it.
6. *Hazlo como te guste* = do it however you like.
7. *Haz lo que prefieres* = do what you prefer. *Haz lo que prefieras* = do whatever you prefer.
8. *Comes lo que prefieras* (statement, present) sounds softer/sweeter than the order *come lo que prefieras*. The order swaps *comes* → *come* (drop the *s*).
9. Formal version of the order uses the mood tense for the command verb too: *coma lo que prefiera* (formal "you eat whatever you prefer"). Plural: *coman lo que prefieran*.
10. **Cuando + future event → mood tense** (review). *Cuando necesites* = whenever/when you need.
11. *Elegir* (e → i): *elijo, eliges, elige, elegimos, eligen*.
12. *Seguir* = to follow / continue / carry on. Same e → i pattern: *sigo, sigues, sigue, seguimos, siguen*. The *e* doesn't split — it turns into *i* when stressed.
13. **Spelling note**: *seguir* → *sigo* (gu becomes g before o/a to keep the hard sound).
14. **Family of *seguir***: *conseguir* = to get / manage to (*consigo*); *perseguir* = to chase (*persigo*). Same irregularity carries over.
15. *Adonde* = to where. With *vayas* (mood form of *ir*): *Te sigo adonde vayas* = I'll follow you wherever you go.
16. **Mood-tense base of *ser*** is *sea* (s-e-a). Forms: *sea, seas, sea, seamos, sean*.
17. *O sea* = literally "or it might be"; used as a filler like "I mean..." / "uhh...".
18. *Así* = like this / like that.
19. *Prefiero que sea así* = I prefer it to be like this.
20. *Sea lo que sea* = whatever it is / whatever will be.

## Examples / Sentences
- *Hazlo.* — Do it.
- *Hazlo donde quieras.* — Do it wherever you want.
- *Hazlo como te guste.* — Do it however you like.
- *Haz lo que prefieras.* — Do whatever you prefer.
- *Come lo que prefieras.* — Eat whatever you prefer. (informal)
- *Coma lo que prefiera.* — Eat whatever you prefer. (formal)
- *Coman lo que prefieran.* — Eat (you all) whatever you prefer.
- *Cuando necesites.* — Whenever you need.
- *Te sigo adonde vayas.* — I'll follow you wherever you go.
- *Prefiero que sea así.* — I prefer it to be like this.
- *Sea lo que sea.* — Whatever will be, will be.
